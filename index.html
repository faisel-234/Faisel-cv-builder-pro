About the Tool

Designed for Those Who Need It Most

Purpose

CV Builder Pro was created to remove one barrier that displaced people, students, and community workers face when applying for jobs and humanitarian programs — the lack of access to professional document creation tools.

Who Can Use It

  • Refugees & displaced persons
  • Students & recent graduates
  • NGO & UN applicants
  • Community volunteers
  • Job seekers & career changers

Benefits

  • Professional NGO-standard formatting
  • Instant PDF generation
  • No registration required
  • Works on mobile & desktop
  • Completely free to use
